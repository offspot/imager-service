Using your hotspot

Once the image has been downloaded, flash it onto a microSD card (we recommend Raspberry Pi Imager, but any such tool will do).

Insert it into your Raspberry, plug the device and wait for about 20-30 seconds until you see a Wikipedia network appear in your wifi settings.

Connect to it and a captive portal should appear after a few seconds. If not, then open http://wikipedia.hotspot in your favourite browser.

Enjoy!
