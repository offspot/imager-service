Using your hotspot

Once the image has been downloaded, flash it onto a microSD card (we recommend Raspberry Pi Imager, but any such tool will do).

Insert the device into your Raspberry Pi, plug it in, and wait for approximately 1-2 minutes during the initial boot-up process until you see the network status display 'Ready' (or Wikipedia if you purchased the Wikipedia-only configuration) in your wifi settings.

Connect to it and a captive portal should appear after a few seconds. If not, then open http://ready.hotspot (or http://wikipedia.hotspot) in your favourite browser.

Enjoy!
