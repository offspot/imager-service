Using your hotspot

Once the image has been downloaded, flash it onto a microSD card (we recommend Raspberry Pi Imager, but any such tool will do).

Insert the card into your Raspberry Pi, plug it in, and wait for approximately 1-2 minutes during the initial boot-up process until you see the network status display Ready (or Wikipedia if you purchased Wikipedia image) appear in your wifi settings.

Connect to it and a captive portal should appear after a few seconds. If not, then open http://goto.kiwix.hotspot in your favourite browser.

Enjoy!

 

Once the download has started you can go back to kiwix.org.
